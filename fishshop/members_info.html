Premium Membership

What does it mean to be a premium member of the Aquatic Centre?

Premium Membership privileges include eligibility to benefit from discounts of up to 10% on listed fish variety and up to 5% on all fish food. Other privileges include:

  • Subscription to the monthly newsletter
  • Participating in annual contests:
    • Rare Breading Species Competition
    • Tank of the year competition
    • Photo Contest

One can get his Premium Membership card at our shop at a cost of € 10, and will be valid for 360 days.

Free Membership

What does it mean to be a free member of the Aquatic Centre?

Free Membership privileges include eligibility to benefit from discounts of up to 5% on listed fish variety when spending € 25 or more. Other privileges include:

  • Subscription to the monthly newsletter
  • Participating in annual contests:
    • Rare Breading Species Competition

One can subscribe for his Free Membership on this page, and will be valid for 360 days.
